About Me

1.35 million people and 70+ billion hours—Annually, 1.35 million people globally lose their lives while driving, and humans spend over 70 billion hours behind the wheel. These staggering figures underline why I chose to study Autonomous Vehicle Engineering.

Passionate about autonomous vehicles because, at its core, it's about creating a world where every goodbye at the driveway isn't shadowed by worry, and every hello is just a safe drive away. It’s about reclaiming those lost moments stuck in traffic and turning them into stories shared, songs sung, and laughter that fills the car. This is my way of helping us all drive into a future where technology serves not just our needs, but our humanity.

Over the last few years, I’ve been deeply immersed in robotics—tinkering, troubleshooting, and innovating to push the boundaries of what’s possible.
